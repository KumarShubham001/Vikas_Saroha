about the rides

Kashmir to Kanyakumari by bicycle

This ride is an attempt at the Guinness World Record for distance against time to complete the journey between Leh and Kanyakumari (a total of around 3950 kms) in under 12 days (288 hours).

Start point : Leh, Kashmir

End point : Kanyakumari
